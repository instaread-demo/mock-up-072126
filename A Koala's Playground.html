Romance C-drama Overdo with Zhang Ling He and Wang Chu Ran Opens to Scathing First Impression Reviews with a Litany of Complaints

A Koala's Playground Posted on July 20, 2026 by ockoalaJuly 20, 2026
Listen to audio version of this article
Sponsored by
0:00 / 1:09
Instaread

The C-drama Overdo with Wang Chu Ran and Zhang Ling He dropped on Sunday the 19th and its a testament to how much buzz was going into the drama that the first reviews are this strong in sentiment. Sadly it’s a negative wave of sentiment as the first impressions are overwhelming a mass of complaints. The AI created opening sequence is universally mocked and hated, the drama filters are dialed up to overkill on every single person onscreen, the director keeps focusing on close ups and cross cutting between characters in a scene, the special efforts are comedically bad including CGI backdrops, and quality of the entire production feels like a mini drama level. There are even hilarious accusations to audit the production for embezzlement because where did the big budget money go because it’s clearly not seen in the end product. The drama has just premiered and so far the viewership is strong but all signs point to a steep drop to come if indeed these early reviews are true and the drama sucks way more than it should. Alas, what a waste of two visual tops paired together.
